Message

Education system in our country and specially in sindh province is very poor and government school fails to provide basic education to kids. Keeping this facts in mind Friends of Memon Welfare Society established two model schools in Jati and Mirpur Bhatoro and providing free education. More than 100 kids including girls getting free basic education , we also provide free uniforms and books to encourage them. Total monthly expenses are around 25 thousand, we need your continuous Financial support to run it in smooth mode. We welcome all our well wishers and donors to come and visit to see it.
